About Wisdom Trace

Wisdom Trace is a living, map-based archive dedicated to collecting and visualizing the insights of human beings across the globe. Every heart pinned on this map carries a trace of a life — its joys, its struggles, and the quiet lessons learned along the way.

The project was born from a simple conviction: wisdom is not locked inside books or institutions. It lives in people — in grandmothers, in students, in strangers on street corners.

"Leave a trace. Let your insight outlive the moment."
